Design Trends

Web Design
Web design involves crafting the visual and interactive elements of websites to create an engaging and user-friendly experience. It begins with the layout and structure, where wireframing and grid systems are used to plan the site's blueprint, ensuring consistency and alignment. Visual design is crucial, involving the selection of color schemes, typography, and imagery to enhance the site's appeal and readability, along with careful consideration of spacing and padding for aesthetic balance.

Animation Design
At the heart of animation design is the concept of movement. Designers create sequences of images that simulate motion when played in succession. This can range from simple animations like a bouncing ball to complex scenes involving multiple characters and intricate backgrounds. The process often starts with storyboarding, where key frames are sketched to outline the major scenes and actions.

Photoshop Media
Photoshop media design encompasses the creation and manipulation of visual elements using Adobe Photoshop, a powerful graphic design and photo editing software. This process involves working with various types of media such as photos, illustrations, and text to produce compelling visual content. Designers utilize Photoshop to enhance and retouch images, apply filters and effects, and create composite images by combining multiple elements.
